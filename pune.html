Top 5 Famous Places to Visit in Pune

Pune is a major city in Maharashtra, India, known as the “Oxford of the East” for its many educational institutions. It has a rich history linked to the Maratha Empire and is now a leading IT and industrial hub. The city is famous for its pleasant climate, cultural heritage, Ganesh Festival, and popular places like Shaniwar Wada and Aga Khan Palace.

Top 5 Tourist Places

  1. Shaniwar Wada

    Shaniwar Wada is a historic fort in Pune, Maharashtra, built in 1732 by the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire. It was the main seat of the Peshwa rulers and is known for its grand architecture and strong walls. Today, it is a famous tourist attraction and an important symbol of Maratha history.

    • Famous for Maratha History
    • Light and Sound Show available
    • Best time: Evening

    Learn more about Shaniwar Wada

  2. Aga Khan Palace

    Aga Khan Palace is a famous historical landmark in Pune, Maharashtra, built in 1892 by Sultan Muhammed Shah Aga Khan. It is known for its beautiful architecture and large gardens. The palace is important in Indian history because Mahatma Gandhi, Kasturba Gandhi, and Mahadev Desai were kept here during the freedom struggle.

    • Peaceful garden
    • Mahatma Gandhi Memorial
    • Historical place

    Learn more about Aga Khan Palace

  3. Sinhagad Fort

    Sinhagad Fort is a famous historical fort located near Pune, Maharashtra. It was an important fort of the Maratha Empire and is known for the brave battle of Tanaji Malusare in 1670. Today, it is a popular tourist and trekking destination, known for its views and historical importance.

    • Best for trekking
    • Beautiful sunrise and sunset
    • Fresh air and nature

    Learn more about Sinhagad Fort

  4. Dagdusheth Ganpati Temple

    Shreemant Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Temple is a famous and revered temple in Pune, Maharashtra, dedicated to Lord Ganesha. It is known for its beautiful idol decorated with gold ornaments. The temple is an important center during the Ganesh Festival and attracts thousands of devotees and tourists every year.

    • Very famous during Ganesh Festival
    • Spiritual place
    • Located in city center

    Learn more about Dagdusheth Temple

  5. Osho Garden

    Osho Garden is a peaceful and beautiful garden located in Pune, Maharashtra, near the Osho Ashram. It is known for its greenery, small streams, and calm environment, making it a perfect place for relaxation and meditation.

    • Calm and silent place
    • Beautiful nature
    • Best for relaxation

    Learn more about Osho Garden
